Project Initiator / Master's Project

May 2023 - July 2023

EU-focus / Germany / The Netherlands

Driven by a passion for urban planning and the potential of active citizen participation, I embarked on a journey to create SmoothRide, a web application aimed at enhancing citizen engagement in shaping city infrastructures. SmoothRide empowers individuals to upload images of their local cycling environments, get AI-powered suggestions on improvements, and share their insights with the broader community and local authorities.

Project Highlights:

  • Developed a user-friendly platform allowing citizens to voice their experiences and insights on cycling infrastructure.
  • Integrated object detection and AI-generated advice for infrastructure improvement suggestions.
  • Ensured data security with a self-hosted database in Pocketbase and crafted the frontend using VUEjs and backend with Flask.
  • Incorporated features like automated Twitter sharing, content recommendation, and integrated insights from the ADFC Fahrradklimareport 2022 based on user feedback.
  • Engaged in real-world user testing, academic paper writing, and iteratively refined the platform based on feedback and data-driven insights.

The Vision:

My aspiration for SmoothRide is to evolve it into a platform serving not just citizens but also urban planners and local authorities. The goal is to foster a participatory approach to urban planning, backed by credible literature, real-world examples, and localized resources. SmoothRide is more than just a tool; it's a testament to the power of citizen-AI collaboration in shaping urban futures.
